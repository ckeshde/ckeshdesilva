Play & Blossom Pediatric Speech Pathology

Helping families connect with care through a modern digital presence.

⊕ Live Website | </> GitHub

Project Overview

I designed and developed a responsive static website for a newly established pediatric speech pathologist. The client needed a digital presence to promote her services, build credibility, and make it easy for potential clients primarily parents to reach out.

The site delivers a professional, approachable feel with a clean layout, soft color palette, and clear calls to action. It’s optimized for both desktop and mobile, ensuring accessibility and trustworthiness across all touchpoints.

My Role: UI/UX Designer & Front-end Developer
Client: Tharasi Seneviratne (Founder of Play & Blossom Pediatric Speech Pathology)
Tools & Stack: Figma / HTML5 / Tailwind CSS
Year: 2025

Target Audience

Parents of young children seeking trusted, approachable, and professional speech therapy services. The site was designed to make it easy to understand the services offered, view credentials, and quickly get in touch.

Key Frontend Features

  • Responsive Design: Built with a mobile-first approach using Tailwind CSS to ensure a smooth experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile.
  • Accessibility & SEO-Friendly Structure: Used semantic HTML to improve accessibility and support better visibility in local search results.
  • Consistent Design System: Used reusable utility classes and structured components to maintain consistent spacing, typography, and colors.
  • Clear CTA: Strategically placed “Enquire Now” buttons across key sections to guide users toward contacting the therapist.
  • Optimized Page Load: LDeveloped as a lightweight static website with optimized images for quick loading.
  • Custom Components: Created modular sections (Hero, Services, Testimonials, Contact) to allow easy updates and future expansion.

Typography

Based on the client’s requirements, two fonts were used for titles and subtitles [Sifonn and Lovelo] to create a distinctive and visually engaging heading style. For body text, Poppins was selected to ensure clarity, readability, and a modern look across the website.

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Entering a competitive local market with no existing brand presence.
Solution: Focused on trust-building visuals, clear credentials, and structured content hierarchy to guide parents confidently toward booking a consultation.

Challenge: No initial brand assets or website content.
Solution: Worked closely with the client to define tone of voice, structure service information clearly, and select soft, welcoming visuals aligned with pediatric care.

Outcome

The website provided the client with her first professional online presence. Within weeks of launch, the site began generating inquiries from parents through local search, helping establish credibility and visibility in a competitive market.
