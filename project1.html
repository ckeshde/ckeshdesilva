Bento-Ya Speech Pathology Website

A complete UI/UX design and front-end development project for a pediatric speech pathology business. The goal was to establish a modern online presence that helps attract clients and improves enquiry flow.

Context

I designed and developed a responsive static website for a newly established pediatric speech pathology brand. The goal was to create a modern online presence that clearly communicates the brand’s identity, showcases services, and improves overall user experience.

Role: UI/UX Designer & Front-end Developer
Client: Thomas (Bento-Ya Founder)
Tools: Figma, HTML, Tailwind CSS

Year: 2025

Problem Statement

The client, a newly established pediatric speech pathologist, needed an online presence to promote her services and connect with potential clients. At the time, she did not have a website to showcase her expertise or collect client enquiries efficiently.

The primary target audience was parents of children who require speech therapy, seeking trustworthy, approachable, and professional support for their child’s communication development.

The main challenge was to stand out in a highly competitive local market where several similar businesses already had professional websites. The goal was to design a visually appealing, user-friendly, and credible website that helps attract families, build trust, and encourage enquiries through a clear call-to-action.
