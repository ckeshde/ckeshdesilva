Plugpin – EV Charging Station Finder App

A mobile app concept that helps electric vehicle users find nearby charging stations with real-time availability and intuitive navigation.

View Brand Guidelines ↗

Project Overview

Plugpin is a mobile application designed to simplify how EV users locate and access charging stations. It focuses on providing a seamless user experience through clear navigation, minimal interface, and strong visual branding. The app design emphasizes sustainability, accessibility, and trust.

The site delivers a professional, approachable feel with a clean layout, soft color palette, and clear calls to action. It’s optimized for both desktop and mobile, ensuring accessibility and trustworthiness across all touchpoints.

My Role: UX + UI Design, Visual design, Branding
Client: Thomas (Founder of Bento-Ya), on behalf of pediatric speech pathologist
Tools & Stack: Figma / HTML5 / Tailwind CSS
Year: 2025

Target Audience

Parents of young children seeking trusted, approachable, and professional speech therapy services. The site was designed to make it easy to understand the services offered, view credentials, and quickly get in touch.

Key Frontend Features

  • Responsive Design: Built with a mobile-first approach using Tailwind CSS for optimal experience across all devices.
  • Semantic HTML Structure: Ensures accessibility and SEO-friendliness for local search discovery.
  • Modular Utility Classes: Speeds up development and maintains consistent styling across components.
  • Clear CTA Placement: Strategically placed “Book a Consultation” and contact buttons to encourage conversions.
  • Optimized Page Load: Lightweight static site with compressed assets for faster load speeds.
  • Custom Components: Built reusable sections like Hero, Services, Testimonials, and Contact for future scalability.

Design Showcase

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Competing in a saturated local market with limited brand recognition.
Solution: Emphasized trust-building visuals and structured content hierarchy to guide users smoothly toward the CTA.

Challenge: Lack of initial brand assets or web content.
Solution: Collaborated with the client to create copy and select brand-aligned imagery for a cohesive and professional look.

Outcome

The client now has a modern, conversion-optimized online presence tailored to her niche. Within the first few weeks of launch, the site helped attract inquiries from families who found her services through local search — a significant improvement from having no web presence at all.
