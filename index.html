About Me

I’m a frontend developer and UI/UX designer with over 5 years of industry experience creating modern, responsive, and user-friendly digital products. My expertise spans from building pixel-perfect web interfaces with clean code to designing intuitive user journeys that balance both aesthetics and functionality.

Over the years, I’ve worked on projects ranging from startup websites to enterprise-level applications, collaborating closely with cross-functional teams to turn ideas into impactful solutions. I’m passionate about crafting experiences that not only look good but also solve real problems for users.

Currently based in Melbourne, I enjoy working in dynamic environments where creativity and technology meet. Outside of work, I stay curious by exploring new design trends, experimenting with emerging frameworks, and continuously sharpening my skills.
